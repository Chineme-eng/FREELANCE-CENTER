Outreach System

Scripts · Pipeline · Follow-ups · Email templates

Your overall beginner cold-call → close rate is 2–5%. At 20 calls/day, expect 1–3 interested leads per day. Consistency is everything.
Opening ScriptFor businesses with no website
Cold call opener — no website
Hey, is this [Business Name]? Hey! My name is KP — I'm a local web designer here in the area. Quick question: do you currently have a website for your business? [If NO:] Got it. I actually build simple, mobile-friendly websites for local businesses — the kind that help you get more calls and bookings from Google. I could even show you a free preview of what your business could look like online. Would that be worth 2 minutes? [If they say yes/sure:] Perfect. Can I get your email or text number so I can send you a quick mockup? It's totally free — no commitment at all. [If they say they're busy:] No worries at all. Can I send you a quick text with an example so you can see what I mean? Takes 10 seconds to look at.
Follow-Up CallAfter sending demo
Day 1 follow-up
Hey [Name], it's KP — the web designer I texted yesterday. Did you get a chance to check out that preview I sent over for [Business Name]? [If yes:] What did you think? I can have a full version live in 2–3 days. I'm doing a few local businesses this week for $[price] — want to lock it in? [If no:] No worries — let me send it again real quick. It's just a mockup of what your business could look like online. Takes 30 seconds to look at. [Key goal:] Never end the call without getting either a YES to the demo, or a specific time to follow up again.
Closing ScriptWhen they're interested
Closing the deal
So based on what you told me, I'd build you: — A clean, mobile-friendly homepage — Your services listed clearly — A contact form / booking button — Google Maps integration — Basic SEO so you show up locally I usually charge $[price] for this. I do 50% upfront, 50% when it goes live. I can have it ready in 2–3 days. Does that work for you? [If they hesitate on price:] Think about it this way — if this site brings in just 1–2 extra customers a month, it pays for itself and keeps paying. And I'm local, so you can actually reach me. [If they say they'll think about it:] Totally fair. Can I check back with you on [specific day]? I'll hold your spot until then.

Scaling Roadmap

From first client to $20K/month — stage by stage

The key lever is not perfecting websites. It's increasing conversations per week + follow-up consistency. Everything else is secondary.
🟦 STAGE 1 — Beginner ($0–$2K/month)
Calls/day: 15–25 Calls/month: 300–500 Clients/month: 1–4 Close rate: 2–5%
Focus: Learn objections · Build 1–2 demo sites/week · Follow up aggressively · Manual calls only
🟨 STAGE 2 — Validation ($2K–$5K/month)
Calls/day: 20–30 Calls/month: 400–600 Clients/month: 3–6 Close rate: 5–8%
What changes: Better scripts · Relume templates · Repeatable demos · Follow-ups become the main driver
🟧 STAGE 3 — Systemized ($5K–$10K/month)
Calls/day: 25–50 Calls/month: 600–1,000 Clients/month: 6–12 Close rate: 8–12%
What changes: Reusable design system · Partial outsourcing · Niche specialization · Start retainers
🟥 STAGE 4 — Semi-Agency ($10K–$20K/month)
Calls/day: 30–60 or mixed inbound Clients/month: 10–25 Close rate: 10–15% Retainers: $150–$300/mo
What changes: Freelancers for fulfillment · SOPs · CRM pipeline · Sales closer · Referrals replace cold calls

📅 Day 1 — Setup + First Outreach
  • →
    Pick ONE niche: HVAC / barbershops / cleaners
  • →
    Find 40–60 leads on Google Maps (no website or outdated only)
  • →
    Build 1 demo site in Wix/Framer for that niche
  • →
    Make 20–25 calls — don't sell, just get "can I show you something?"
Target: 2–5 interested leads · 1–2 "send it over" replies
📅 Day 2 — First Demo Push
  • →
    Build or refine 1 demo site (same niche style)
  • →
    Send demo to all interested leads from Day 1
  • →
    Make 20–30 new calls
Target: 3–8 warm leads · 1–2 follow-up conversations started
📅 Day 3–4 — Closing Attempts Begin
  • →
    Follow up ALL Day 1–2 leads. Push urgency: "I can build this for $500+ in a few days"
  • →
    25–30 new calls each day
  • →
    Improve demo — add testimonials section, stronger hero
Target: 1–2 serious buyers · 1 possible close
📅 Day 5 — First Close Day
  • →
    Follow up ALL leads aggressively. Stop pitching — start closing.
  • →
    Offer: "$500+ website, 3–5 day delivery. 50% now, 50% on launch."
  • →
    20–25 new calls
Target: 1 client closed (if execution is solid)
📅 Day 6–7 — Momentum + Pipeline Lock
  • →
    Call fresh 40–60 leads + re-hit old ones
  • →
    Use proof: "I just started building sites for local businesses like yours"
  • →
    Follow up EVERYONE on Day 7 — close remaining warm leads
Target: 2–3 total clients closed OR 2 clients + 3–5 warm pipeline leads
You don't sell websites. You sell: HVAC → "more calls/month" · Barbers → "more bookings" · Salons → "fully booked calendar" · Mechanics → "steady local customers"
